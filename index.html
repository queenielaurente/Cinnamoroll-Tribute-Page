Meet Cinnamoroll

Cinnamoroll is Cafe Cinnamon's official mascot. He is quite shy, but very friendly, and sometimes takes a nap on customers' laps. He can fly through the air by flapping his huge ears. He works at Cafe Cinnamon, and sometimes sleeps on customers laps.
